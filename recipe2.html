Spicy Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice
  • 800g chicken thighs, boneless
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 tbsp yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 inch ginger, grated
  • 4 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 cloves
  • A pinch of saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and soak it for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat 2 tbsp of ghee. Add the sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Remove half and set aside.
  3. Add cumin seeds, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves to the pot. Saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they start to change color.
  6. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  7. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  8. Add yogurt and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
  9. In another pot, bring water to boil. Add salt and the drained rice. Cook until 70% done.
  10. Drain the rice and layer it over the chicken mixture.
  11. Sprinkle the reserved fried onions, saffron milk, and chopped coriander and mint.
  12. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  13. Mix gently before serving.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (serves 6):

  • Calories: 580
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 70g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Sodium: 570mg
